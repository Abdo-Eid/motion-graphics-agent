# Project Aurora — Internal Brief

## Goal

Produce a 30-second product explainer for Project Aurora, a tool that helps small teams convert raw meeting notes into structured action items.

## Audience

Engineering managers and product leads at companies of 10 to 200 people who are drowning in unstructured meeting notes.

## Tone

Calm, confident, and slightly playful. Avoid corporate jargon. Avoid superlatives.

## Key Messages

1. Meeting notes lose value within 24 hours if no one acts on them.
2. Aurora extracts owners, deadlines, and decisions automatically.
3. Aurora integrates with the tools the team already uses (Linear, Notion, Slack).

## Visual Direction

- Color palette: deep navy `#0B1220`, soft cyan `#67E8F9`, warm off-white `#F8FAFC`.
- Typography: Inter for body, JetBrains Mono for code captions.
- Mood: focused, modern, low-motion. No bouncing icons.

## Reference Stat

A 2024 internal survey of 312 engineering managers found that 71 percent of action items mentioned in meetings never get tracked anywhere outside the meeting itself.
